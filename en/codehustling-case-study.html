CodeHustling

Central hub for a web developer community of 185k+ members

WordPress Community Platform SEO

Project Overview

Client

CodeHustling is a website built for an Instagram community led by Fabion Kallaku, who publishes content about web development and the web industry. With over 185,000 followers, his community is highly engaged and active.

Goal

Create a centralized, informative website that provides comprehensive details about Fabion and his Instagram community. The goal was to have all information in one place to improve accessibility and engagement.

Challenges

Information Centralization

It was necessary to consolidate scattered community information from multiple platforms into one cohesive hub.

Community Engagement

Maintaining high engagement levels during the followers' transition to the new platform.

Hosting Setup

Required reliable hosting and domain management for a growing community.

Solutions

Content Strategy

Developed a comprehensive content architecture managing all community resources and information.

Engagement Features

Implemented interactive elements and clear CTAs to encourage community participation.

Hosting & Domain

Set up enterprise-grade hosting with managed domain services for optimal performance.

Development Process

1

Discovery Call

Initial consultations to understand goals and expectations for the community platform.

2

Market Analysis

Researching web developer communities and analyzing competitor platforms.

3

Design & Branding

Designing and refining the website layout using Figma, focusing on community-centered design.

4

Development

Building a custom website optimized for content updates and community features.

5

SEO Optimization

Implementing comprehensive SEO strategies to increase visibility and organic reach.

6

Launch & Maintenance

Launching the site with ongoing monthly maintenance for smooth operation.

Results

The website effectively centralized all community information, facilitating easier access to relevant content for followers and increasing brand credibility.
