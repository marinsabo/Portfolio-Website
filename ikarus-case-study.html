Complete Rebranding & Web Design for Digital Agency

Branding Web Design Conversion

Project Overview

Client

Ikarus Agency is a digital marketing agency specializing in helping businesses enhance their online presence through strategic branding, web design, and digital marketing solutions.

Goal

Complete rebranding and website redesign to establish Ikarus as a premium digital agency and increase client conversions through improved UX and compelling visual design.

Challenges

Brand Identity

Needed to establish strong brand identity that differentiated them in competitive agency market.

Credibility

Required premium design to attract higher-value clients and compete with established agencies.

Conversion

Previous website had low conversion rates and failed to showcase agency capabilities effectively.

Solutions

Strategic Branding

Developed comprehensive brand guidelines with modern logo, color palette, and typography system.

Premium Design

Created sophisticated, conversion-focused design showcasing services and case studies.

UX Optimization

Streamlined user journeys with clear CTAs and intuitive navigation to boost conversions.

Development Process

1

Brand Discovery

Deep dive into agency values, target audience, and competitive positioning.

2

Brand Development

Created comprehensive brand identity including logo, colors, and visual system.

3

Website Design

Designed modern website in Figma with focus on conversions and premium feel.

4

Development

Built responsive website optimized for performance and user experience.

5

Launch

Deployed with full branding rollout across all digital touchpoints.
